TO COMPLETE THE SETUP:

Please save your father's photo (the image you shared in the chat showing him with Ella) as:
  images/deda.jpg

Steps:
1. Right-click the image you shared in the chat (father with Ella in the kitchen)
2. Save it to your computer
3. Save or move the file to: Mommy-Card/images/deda.jpg
4. The Get Well card will automatically display this image

The photo will appear in the middle of the "Get Well Soon" card, replacing the upload button.

Alternative formats supported:
- images/deda.jpg (preferred)
- images/deda.png

Note: If the image file is not found, the card will show an upload button instead.
You can also click on the displayed image to change it temporarily.
